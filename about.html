We are the BIGGEST Fun & Interactive Learning experience platform for children and family.

Olimpya Kids is the biggest Fun & Interactive Learning experience platform for children and family. Facilitated with active learning programs to support children exploration and discovery abilities, Olimpya Kids also aim to boost engaging, social and natural interactions between children and their surroundings. It is created and driven by valuing each children growth stages in mind.

We will provide our continuous support and assistance for children on each of their growth stages so they could Discover, Explore and Grow their potential and max out their competencies to overcome the future challenges.

This platform is developed by Experia – The Event Marketing Company.

Olimpya Kids to be the first and leading Children Fun-Interactive-Learning experience platform in Indonesia.

Olimpya Kids will bring out all the goodness of education and combine it with the joy and wonder inside the play-learn-compete arena.

This platform will also be particularly special as it become a space where children could interact with friends and families, and be encouraged to enjoy and explore all the fun activities.

Olimpya Kids is committed to promote Indonesia's children activities, and further serves to ensure the effectiveness and significance of this platform by working closely with respective stakeholders in academies, governments, children-oriented organization, industry and many more.

Explore

Children searching and inquiring activity focusing to enhance their learning skills and familiarize themselves with the subjects in detail.

Each learning process are designed to align with each children growth phases, so the end result will be optimum and best suits children and parents goals.

Discover

Discover programs are focusing to unlock children hidden talents and potentials through the designed activities and competitions, so the outcome will expand their cognitive aspect and strengthen their self-esteem.

Grow

Programs designed to optimize children awareness to self, to enrich their understandings about themselves as individual and social creatures, as well as their surroundings.

Programs also designed to expand children knowledge and understanding about environment, ethical values and moral conscience, to build and encourage good character, behavior and moral consciousness.
