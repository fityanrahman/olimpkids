We are the BIGGEST Fun & Interactive Learning experience platform for children and family.

Olympia Kids facilitated with active learning programs to support children exploration and discovery abilities. We also aim to boost engaging, social and natural interactions between children and their surroundings. It is created and driven by valuing each children growth stages in mind.

We will provide our continuous support and assistance for children on each of their growth stages so they could Discover, Explore and Grow their potential and max out their competencies to overcome the future challenges.

This platform is developed by Experia – The Event Marketing Company.

Olimpya Kids to be the first and leading platform IN INDONESIA FOR Children TO PROVIDE A FUN AND INTERACTIVE LEARNING EXPERIENCE

WE will bring out all the goodness of education and combine it with the joy and wonder inside the arena.
